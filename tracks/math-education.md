# Preparing to teach mathematics

For students interested in becoming math teachers, Binghamton offers:

- **Undergraduate minor in Education** [School of Education / TLEL]({{BU_TLEL_EDUCATION_MINOR}})
- **Master of Arts in Teaching (MAT)** in [Adolescence Education (includes Mathematics)]({{BU_TLEL_MAT_ADOLESCENCE_EDUCATION}})
  - Either the **BA (Mathematics track)** or **BS (Mathematics track)** can provide strong preparation for this program.

For recommended math coursework and a sample course sequence for future teachers, see: [Math Education Page]({{BU_MATH_EDUCATION_PAGE}}).
